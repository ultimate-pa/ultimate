This folder contains the benchmarks used in the PhD thesis of Eric Koskinen
http://cs.nyu.edu/~ejk/publications.html

The subfolder 
    dpredicates-benchmarks
contains the examples from
    http://www.cl.cam.ac.uk/~ejk39/ltl/
    
The subfolder
    branching-benchmarks
contains the examples from
    http://www.cl.cam.ac.uk/~ejk39/actl/
    
Both URLs are mentioned in the thesis.
At least the benchmarks from the first URL were also used in at least one 
other paper.
